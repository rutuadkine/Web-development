about us

Having been in the business of teaching people how to eat Italian food for almost three decades, the brand, Little Italy is synonymous with the cuisine in India. It exudes the same home-like warmth and distinctiveness that is reflective of how the world perceives Italy. Our journey began in 1989, in Pune, when our founder Mr. Raj Mehta realised the need for a vegetarian Italian fine dining format in India. In the last three decades, Little Italy as a brand has blossomed beyond everyone's expectations, with 35 outlets across 20 cities in 3 countries!
